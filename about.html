Formula 1 (F1) is the pinnacle of global motorsport,
featuring 11 teams and 22 drivers competing in 24 high-speed Grand Prix races across five continents.
It combines elite human talent with advanced,
bespoke, open-wheel cars—reaching speeds over 370kph—
to compete for both a Drivers' and Constructors' World Championship.
