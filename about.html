About Us

Formula 1 (F1) is the highest class of international, open-wheel, single-seater auto racing, often considered the pinnacle of motorsport. It is a team sport—featuring 10 teams and 22 drivers—where bespoke, highly advanced cars compete on city streets and purpose-built tracks worldwide for both Drivers' and Constructors' Championships.
