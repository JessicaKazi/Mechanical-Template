About Us

Formula 1 (F1) is the highest class of international, open-wheel, single-seater auto racing, often considered the pinnacle of motorsport. It is a team sport—featuring 10 teams and 22 drivers—where bespoke, highly advanced cars compete on city streets and purpose-built tracks worldwide for both Drivers' and Constructors' Championships.

Why Choose Formula 1?

Formula 1 offers a thrilling mix of elite speed (over 200 mph),, cutting-edge technology, and high-stakes drama, making it the pinnacle of motorsport. With 24 races across 2026, it offers high-speed, strategic, and often dangerous competition featuring the world's best drivers, providing intense, 2-hour spectacles.
